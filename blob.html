Background

This project was created by me and my research advisor Dr. Eisenlohr. We were exploring different solution schemes for locating peak diffaction maxima in Laue X-ray microdiffaction. After publication I can start releasing the code.

Here is a very nice result, where the red circles represent the predictions made by our program about where a diffraction maxima may be. This is overlayed on top of the diffraction image that is used as input to our program.

This is an example of the peridic nature of microdiffraction of metals! The structure you see in the X-rays refelect the ordered crystalline structure of the metal hit by the X-ray.
